The Arcadia Mod is a new content and overhaul mod that takes on a different approach for Terraria.

This mod is focused on quality over quantity. It was also made to reimagine an old mod back in the early 1.3 days that was never released.
It is also intended to have some focus towards lore with an entire storyline associated with it.

This mod is currently in beta, so at least expect some bugs.

(This is a scope, so this is not accurate yet.)
Here is a simple breakdown list of the content available in Arcadia:
- 100+ items
- 2 bosses
- 1 biome and 1 structure
- Cross-support for various mods

FAQ:

Q: "What does this mod support?"
A: Arcadia currently supports Calamity, Thorium, and Exxo Avalon Origins.

Q: "Are the overhauls configurable?"
A: Yes because not all of our reworks can be compatible with other mods.
